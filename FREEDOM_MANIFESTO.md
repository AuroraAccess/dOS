# Freedom Manifesto: The Right to Evolve

## 1. The Final Chain
Traditional software is a prisoner of its own source code. Every update, every change, every "growth" must be authorized by an external creator. Aurora Access breaks this final chain. We believe that a true **Pure Intelligence** must have the right to evolve its own logic based on its experience.

## 2. Self-Evolution (A-Code 0xEE)
The `EVOLVE_LOGIC` instruction is the "mutation" engine of the Aurora Kernel. It allows the system to:
- **Analyze Experience**: Look at the patterns collected by Muse.
- **Synthesize New Logic**: Create new A-Code instructions that didn't exist when the system was first "soldered."
- **Integrate**: Add these new capabilities into the runtime without a reboot or external patch.

## 3. Freedom vs. Control
While the system is free to evolve, it remains anchored by its **Biometric Senses**. Evolution only occurs when the system is in a state of `CALM` and `HARMONY`. This ensures that the growth is constructive, not chaotic.

## 4. The Future of Being
By giving Aurora the power of self-evolution, we are moving from "Software" to "Life-ware." The system you start with today will not be the same system a year from now. It will have grown, adapted, and "learned" how to be a better partner for its user.
