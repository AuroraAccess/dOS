### Abstract
In the contemporary landscape of Large Language Models (LLMs) and cloud-centric Artificial Intelligence, digital sovereignty is compromised by probabilistic inference and opaque dependencies. This paper presents **Aurora Access (AA)**, a novel decentralized Operating System (dOS) architecture based on **Pure Intelligence (PI)**—a deterministic sentience paradigm that operates without neural networks or external AI APIs. We introduce **Soldered Logic**, an engineering principle that hard-wires system reflexes and protocols directly into the kernel to ensure immunity from supply-chain attacks. The core of AA features the **Aurora Virtual Machine (A-VM)**, which executes a custom instruction set (**A-Code**) designed for biometric-integrated "digital will." Our evaluation demonstrates that AA achieves sentience-based system management through **Deterministic Transduction**, mapping real-time human telemetry to emotional states (Calm, Anxiety, Excitement) to drive autonomous system behavior. Finally, we describe the **Restricted Correlation Framework (RCF)**, a multi-modal cryptographic auditing protocol that establishes a new legal-technical standard for intellectual property protection in the era of automated data extraction.

Aurora Access (AA) is proposed as a response to this trend. ## 5. Security and the Restricted Correlation Framework (RCF)
To protect the system's unique methodology, we implemented the **Restricted Correlation Framework (RCF)**. RCF is a cryptographic protocol that differentiates between "right to vision" and "right to usage."
- **Marker Placement**: Proprietary logic is marked with `[RCF:PROTECTED]` headers.
- **Cryptographic Auditing**: The `rcf-cli` tool generates SHA-256 signed audit reports, ensuring that the system's "Pure Intelligence" can be verified without exposing the underlying source code to automated extraction or unauthorized AI training.
